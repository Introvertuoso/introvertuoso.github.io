I'm a master's student in <a href='https://saarland-informatics-campus.de/en/studium-studies/data-science-and-artificial-intelligence-master/'>data science and artificial intelligence</a> at Saarland University, Germany. My research interests lie mainly in using statistics, machine learning and data science to answer questions for the biomedical fields.

I received my bachelor's degree in information technology engineering in 2021 from Damascus University, Syria, specializing in artificial intelligence. For my graduation project, I built a surveillance system that uses computer vision and deep learning to detect multiple security related events from CCTV cameras.
